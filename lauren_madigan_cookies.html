Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

  • Title
  • Description
  • Ingredients

Description

In this article, you will learn how to make the best choclate chip cookies ever! The secret is some butter and lot of love.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Baking Soda
  • Butterscotch

Directions

  • Mix dry ingredients together
  • Mix in butter and eggs
  • Add chocolate
  • Heat oven to 350
  • Roll cookies into balls
  • bake for five minutes
